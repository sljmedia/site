Bridge Of Weir.

Zibow is a joint venture between Bridge of Weir Leather Company and its Chinese partner, Polygrace, set up to target automotive manufacturers in the Far East. To meet the brief, SLJ Media developed a corporate identity that could reflect brand elements from both companies but would also be distinctive and individual.

Bridge Of Weir.

Bridge of Weir is a major supplier of high quality finished upholstery leather to the automotive, aviation, marine and contract furnishings industries. To present the company’s range of high performance leathers, SLJ Media design and develop integrated corporate and marketing aids targeting customers from some of the world’s leading brands.

Gretna Green
Ltd.

Gretna Green – renowned for runaway weddings since 1754 and one of Scotland’s top visitor attractions – has commissioned SLJ Media to design and install new signage across its site to coincide with a major refurbishment programme. The project provides the opportunity to take a fresh approach with the branding of its famous Blacksmiths Shop and Exhibition, restaurant, retail outlets and other visitor facilities.

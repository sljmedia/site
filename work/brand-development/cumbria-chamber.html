Cumbria Chamber
Of Commerce.

Cumbria Chamber of Commerce is one of the prime movers in stimulating the Cumbrian economy and is recognised as a leading exemplar in the delivery of business support and services. Taking on board the wide range of business activities undertaken by the Chamber, SLJ Media developed an extensive brand portfolio and visual style that would work at a national, regional and local level across all media platforms. This approach has provided the flexibility to develop distinctive marketing and business communications to target and engage specific audiences.

Thistle Hotels.

To support its ambitious expansion plans, Thistle Hotels commissioned SLJ Media to produce a stylish brochure that would provide development partners with an insight to the commercial aims and aspirations of the company. The brochure includes guidelines to help developers design and construct 4-star hotels to the high standards Thistle requires.

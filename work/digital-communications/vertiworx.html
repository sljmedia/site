VertiworX.

www.vertiworx.co.uk

VertiworX is a recently formed CAA licensed operator of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) aiming to develop its aerial inspection, surveying and photography business. SLJ Media have been commissioned to design and develop the VertiworX brand and a fully responsive website to help the new company promote its services across a wide range of industry sectors.

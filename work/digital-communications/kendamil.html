Kendal
Nutricare.

www.kendamil.com

Kendal Nutricare is the leading UK manufacturer of premium quality infant formula and cereals. To coincide with the launch of its own branded products, SLJ Media were chosen to design and build a website to market Kendamil branded whole milk formula and cereals direct to consumers. The fully responsive e-commerce site will not only provide a shop front for the Kendamil range but will also allow consumers to explore the nutritional benefits of the products for babies and growing toddlers.
